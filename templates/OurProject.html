Emotion Detector

Hi there! This is an emotion detector. Given an image, it will localize the objects and tell which class the model thinks they belong to. There are only 5 classes that the model knows. Make sure to adjust your expectations :)

Under the hood, the webpage (front-end) will send your image to the back-end. Then, the model makes detections and sends results for you to see here.
